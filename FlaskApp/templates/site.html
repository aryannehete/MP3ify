MP3ify

Are you a Spotify user who wants to listen offline but does not want to pay for a premium plan?
MP3ify is the solution for you!

MP3ify works by directly downloading .mp3's of your requested songs from YouTube!

Benefits of MP3ify

Fast

MP3ify works quickly, only
taking about 1 minute per
song to download! Other apps
record each song, taking much
longer.

Free

MP3ify is completely free to use!
Other apps such as Sidify require
you to buy the app.

Web-Based

MP3ify requires no download!
Just paste in your link and
download a .zip containing all
your songs!
